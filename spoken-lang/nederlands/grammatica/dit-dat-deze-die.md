# Demonstrative Pronouns

- in english, there is "this" and "that"

| gender | this(near) | that(far) | these | those |
| ------ | ---------- | --------- | ----- | ----- |
| de     | deze       | die       | deze  | die   |
| het    | dit        | dat       | deze  | die   |

* plural words are "de"

- de boom:
  - deze boom(this tree)
  - die boom(that tree)
